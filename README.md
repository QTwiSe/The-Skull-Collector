## 📖 The Storyline Trilogy
### Part 1 (2D): The Transformation
Floating 30cm above the ground, Mr. Perch lives in deep mine shafts, dreaming of becoming human. He collects skulls for their calcium. In the finale, he grinds them down, consumes the powder, and transforms into an anthropomorphic man with a perch’s head.

### Part 2 (2D): The Disappointment
Upon reaching the surface, he is appalled by the cruel human world. He decides to return to his fish form. He hunts for cow skulls in a junkyard on the outskirts. Just as he collects almost enough, he falls into a pit, landing back in his home shafts.

### Part 3 (3D): The Escape
The Human-Perch tries to leave through an old tunnel, but it’s blocked. He searches for the pickaxe from Part 1, but moving the rubble requires immense strength. He collects human skulls, grinds them, and consumes them to gain power. After clearing the path and grinding the remaining cow skulls, he finally transforms back into a pure fish. He returns to the depths, seals the entrance from the inside, and destroys the pickaxe forever.

### 🛠️ Level Mechanics & Emerald Keys

Campaign Structure: 30 levels total, divided into three tiers (1–10, 11–20, 21–30).
The Door Levels (10, 20, 30): Locked. To unlock the final door of a tier, the player must complete at least 7 out of 10 levels in that block.
Emerald Key: Awarded automatically upon completing the 7th level of a tier. Triggers a UI animation: "You have received an Emerald Key!" 🔑
🔄 Endless Mode

Objective: Farm currency to unlock premium content.
Survival Rule: To progress every 20 levels, the player must collect at least 3 Amethysts.
Rewards: Every 10 levels completed grants 5 Blue Buttercups. 🌸

💎 Economy & Skin Shop: "Mr. Carp's Boutique"
Items: In-level pickups vanish after completion, while store-bought items are permanent.
Unique Collections: Skins in this tier grant special abilities.
Currency: Blue Buttercup (🌸).
Conversion: 1 Amethyst (hidden in-level, requires a pickaxe) = 2 Blue Buttercups.
Campaign Cap: 90 Buttercups (30 levels × 3 amethysts × 2 buttercups).
Skin Pricing:
Common: up to 10 🌸
Rare: up to 50 🌸
Mythic: up to 200 🌸

"Mr. Carp's Unique Collection": up to 2,500 🌸 (Includes the TSK 2 Human-Perch skin).
Note: To afford this after the campaign, the player must complete 4,820 levels in Endless Mode. 🎮

## 🍎 Items & Objects
Environment: Platforms resembling punch cards, scattered cakes, cherries, and amethysts.
Cherry 🍒: Restores +1 HP (Max HP: 2).

Cake 🍰: +1 HP + Shield (Blocks one incoming hit).

Lapis Cherry 🫐: +1 HP + Temporary extra heart (Minecraft style).

Pyotr’s Firecracker (TNT) 🧨: Trap. Ignites on contact, explodes after 1.5s. Deals 1 HP damage if caught in the blast.

Amethyst 💎: 3 per level, hidden in secrets. Requires a pickaxe to mine.

Blue Buttercup 🌸: Main currency.
